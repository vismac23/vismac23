Unlocking the Secrets of Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition

The program is designed to provide a common scientific and cultural background for participants, with topics including

  • smart and embedded vision systems,
  • knowledge representation and big data,
  • biometric systems and security,
  • 3D computer vision,
  • deep learning and neural networks,
  • re-identification and camera networks,
  • social networks applications,
  • multimedia for cultural heritage.
